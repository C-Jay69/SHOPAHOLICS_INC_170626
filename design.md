# Shopaholics Inc — Design System

## Brand Voice
Professional, customer-obsessed, with playful sarcasm. Witty UI copy. Never corporate, never cringe.

## Typography
- **Font Family:** Poppins (Google Fonts) — display + body
- **Headings:** Bold (700–900), gradient text (purple→cyan)
- **Body:** 400–500 weight, gray-300 on dark backgrounds
- **Sizes:** text-5xl lg:text-7xl for hero, text-2xl for section heads, text-sm for labels

## Color Palette
- **Background Primary:** #000000 (black)
- **Background Secondary:** #0a0a0a
- **Background Card:** #111111 / #1a1a1a
- **Background Elevated:** #2a2a2a
- **Border:** #1f2937 (gray-800), subtle
- **Text Primary:** #ffffff
- **Text Secondary:** #d1d5db (gray-300)
- **Text Muted:** #6b7280 (gray-500)
- **Accent Purple:** #a855f7 (purple-500)
- **Accent Cyan:** #06b6d4 (cyan-500)
- **Gradient:** from-purple-500 to-cyan-500 (buttons, headings, badges)
- **Gradient Alt:** from-blue-400 to-cyan-400

## Buttons
- **Primary:** bg-gradient-to-r from-purple-500 to-cyan-500, white text, rounded-lg, px-6 py-3
- **Secondary:** border-2 border-white, white text, hover: bg-white text-black, rounded-lg
- **Danger:** bg-red-600, white text
- **Ghost:** transparent, white text, hover: bg-white/10

## Cards
- bg: #111111 or #1a1a1a
- border: 1px solid #1f2937
- border-radius: rounded-xl
- hover: border-purple-500/50, slight lift

## Layout
- Max width: max-w-7xl, centered
- Section padding: py-16 lg:py-24
- Grid: 12-col, lg:grid-cols-2, lg:grid-cols-3, lg:grid-cols-4
- Generous negative space

## Navigation
- Fixed top, bg-black/95 backdrop-blur
- Logo left, links center/right
- Cart icon with count badge (purple)
- Mega-menu for categories on hover

## Special Components
- **Impulse Coach Widget:** Fixed bottom-right, purple glow, toggle on/off
- **Dupe Finder Badge:** On product cards, "£ Cheaper Alt Found" in cyan
- **Gradient Badge:** for "Sale", "New", "AI Pick"

## No-Nos
- No animations (per spec)
- No popups or modals for marketing
- No neon chaos — use gradients tastefully
- No rounded-full on cards
